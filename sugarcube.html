Incidents as Encounters

Let us say that an act of viewing or witnessing a non human activity is an incident. For an incident to be considered an encounter, there has to be a reciprocal reaction. A response from the other being. An encounter is not passive. A dense moment is created when one encounters another being. I am interested in this dense moment.
